Lakshmi heads Training and Placement division at Rishi MS Engineering and Technology College for Women. She has 10+ years of extensive IT industry experience working on Java, Dot net, Database, CRM and Azure cloud technologies. She is certified in Oracle DBA and Azure cloud platforms. Lakshmi has 2 years of training and placement experience in academic space. She leverages her deep IT industry and technology experience to devise effective and relevant technological and soft skill training programs for students. She believes in the holistic development of students with a strong emphasis on result-based outcomes. Internships are an excellent platform to bridge the gap between academics and industry. Rishi considers this as an extremely important aspect in getting a student ‘placement ready’. Lakshmi passionately drives & guides students to take-up internships and participate in innovation hackathons right from the beginning of academics. Her mentoring skills and technology background is a huge factor in significantly higher internship and hackathons participation of students at Rishi.
